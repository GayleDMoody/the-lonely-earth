What kind of stars host Earth-like planets?

Every Earth-like planet we've found orbits a small, cool star — none orbit stars like our Sun.

🔭

Smaller planets are harder to detect with current instruments. The rarity of Earth-like worlds in this data is partly real and partly a limitation of how we find exoplanets.
